# Loqui — The Name

## Origin

**Loqui** (prononciation : /ˈloʊ.kwi/ — "LOH-kwee") est le verbe latin signifiant **"parler"**. C'est un verbe déponent — actif dans le sens, passif dans la forme — une dualité qui reflète parfaitement le produit : l'utilisateur parle activement, mais l'outil reste passif, invisible, en arrière-plan.

## Etymology & Derivatives

Le mot latin *loqui* est la racine d'une famille de mots utilisés dans toutes les langues occidentales :

| Derivative | Meaning | Connection to the product |
|---|---|---|
| **Eloquent** | Who speaks well | Loqui cleans up your speech to make it eloquent |
| **Loquacious** | Talkative | The user speaks freely, without restraint |
| **Colloquy** | A conversation | Loqui fits naturally into any conversation context |
| **Elocution** | The art of speaking clearly | Voice clarity is the core input |
| **Soliloquy** | Speaking to oneself | Dictating alone at your desk — talking to Loqui |
| **Interlocutor** | Someone who speaks in a conversation | Loqui is your silent interlocutor |
| **Ventriloquism** | Speaking from the belly | Text appears as if typed by invisible hands |

## Why This Name Works

### 1. Abstract yet meaningful
Most people won't recognize "loqui" as Latin — it feels like a unique, invented brand name. But those who know (or look it up) discover a rich, intentional meaning. This creates a "discovery moment" that deepens brand attachment.

### 2. Short & memorable
5 letters. Two syllables. Easy to spell, easy to say, easy to Google. `loqui.app`, `getloqui.com`, `@loqui` — all formats work.

### 3. Internationally pronounceable
The sounds /l/, /o/, /k/, /w/, /i/ exist in virtually every language. No difficult consonant clusters, no ambiguous vowels.

### 4. No direct competitor conflict
There is no major tech product named "Loqui". The name is distinctive and ownable.

### 5. Premium & intellectual feel
Latin carries connotations of education, precision, and timelessness — aligned with a tool designed for professionals who care about the quality of their written communication.

### 6. Verb identity
Loqui is a verb — an action. The product IS an action: you speak, text appears. The name doesn't describe a thing, it describes what you do.

## Brand Story (short version)

> The ancient Romans had a word for the purest form of speaking — not shouting, not whispering, just speaking. *Loqui.* Today, Loqui brings that simplicity back: hold a key, speak naturally, and your words appear as polished text, right where you need them. No cloud. No friction. Just you, speaking.
