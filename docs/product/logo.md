# Loqui — Logo Brief

This document describes the visual identity direction for the Loqui logo. Use this as a brief for a designer or AI image generator.

---

## Brand Attributes

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| **Personality** | Minimal, elegant, invisible-until-needed, friendly |
| **Feel** | Premium but approachable. Not corporate, not playful — refined. |
| **Keywords** | Voice, speech, text, local, private, effortless, always-on |
| **Color mood** | Clean, neutral with a single accent. Not loud. |

---

## Logo Concept Directions

### Direction A — Abstract Speech Mark (Recommended)

A minimal, geometric mark that subtly evokes speech or sound without being a literal speech bubble or microphone.

**Description:**
- Two or three concentric arcs (like sound waves emanating from a point) combined with a text cursor or pen nib
- The arcs represent the voice; the cursor represents the text output
- Ultra clean, single weight, works at 16x16 favicon size
- Think: the intersection between a sound wave and a blinking text cursor

**Prompt for AI generation:**
> Minimal geometric logo mark for "Loqui", a voice-to-text desktop app. Abstract sound waves (2-3 concentric arcs) merging with a text cursor symbol. Clean lines, single stroke weight, monochrome black on white. No text in the mark. Modern, refined, works at small sizes. Flat design, no gradients, no shadows.

---

### Direction B — Stylized "L" Monogram

The letter "L" from Loqui, designed to incorporate a subtle speech or wave element.

**Description:**
- A geometric "L" where the vertical stroke subtly curves or radiates like a sound wave at the top
- Or: the horizontal stroke of the "L" transforms into a flowing wave/ripple
- Minimal, sans-serif foundation
- Works as a standalone app icon

**Prompt for AI generation:**
> Minimal monogram logo for "Loqui". The letter "L" in a clean sans-serif style, where the top of the vertical stroke radiates subtle sound wave arcs. Monochrome, flat design, geometric, modern. Works as a macOS app icon (rounded square). No additional text. Simple enough for a favicon.

---

### Direction C — Quotation Mark / Speech Symbol

Playing on the idea that Loqui captures spoken words — using a stylized quotation mark or opening bracket.

**Description:**
- An elegant, oversized opening quotation mark (« or ") rendered in a modern geometric style
- Represents the beginning of speech — the moment you start talking
- Could incorporate a subtle wave or vibration effect in the curves
- Clean, distinctive, immediately recognizable

**Prompt for AI generation:**
> Minimal logo mark for "Loqui", a voice-to-text app. A single stylized opening quotation mark in clean geometric design, with subtle sound wave vibration in the curves. Monochrome, flat, modern. Works at small sizes. No text, just the symbol. Refined and premium feel.

## Color Palette

### Primary (for the mark)

| Usage | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Dark mark (default) | Near-black | `#1A1A18` |
| Light mark (on dark bg) | Off-white | `#FAFAF8` |
| Accent (optional highlight) | Blue | `#2563EB` |

### App Icon Background

For macOS rounded-square app icon:
- **Option 1:** White/off-white background (`#FAFAF8`) with dark mark — clean, Apple-like
- **Option 2:** Dark background (`#1A1A18`) with light mark — bold, stands out in Dock
- **Option 3:** Accent blue background (`#2563EB`) with white mark — distinctive

---

## Typography (Wordmark)

If a wordmark (the word "Loqui" written out) is needed alongside the mark:

- **Font family:** A clean geometric sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans, or similar)
- **Weight:** Medium (500) — not too bold, not too thin
- **Letter spacing:** Slightly loose (+2-4%)
- **Case:** Capitalized first letter only ("Loqui") — not all-caps, not all-lowercase
- **Style note:** The "q" descender can be a subtle design element — consider a custom "q" that references the brand mark

---

## Usage Specifications

### macOS App Icon
- Rounded square (Apple's standard mask)
- Mark centered with generous padding (~30% margin)
- Single color mark on solid background
- Must be legible at 16x16, 32x32, 128x128, 512x512, 1024x1024

### Favicon
- Simplified version of the mark (fewer details)
- Must work at 16x16 and 32x32
- Consider using just the core shape without any fine details

### Menu Bar / System Tray
- Monochrome (template image on macOS)
- 18x18 or 22x22 logical pixels
- Must match system appearance (dark/light)

### Widget
- The widget currently shows a colored pill/bar
- The logo mark could replace or complement the idle state icon
- Must be recognizable at very small sizes (8-12px height)

---

## What to Avoid

- Microphone icons (overused, too literal)
- Speech bubbles (too "chat app")
- Headphone/earphone imagery (wrong direction — this is about output, not input)
- Waveform/audio spectrum (too music-app)
- Gradients or complex color schemes (keep it flat and minimal)
- Overly decorative or ornamental design (conflicts with "invisible" brand personality)
- Latin/Roman aesthetic (don't lean into classical imagery — the name is enough)
